Purpose

This site is a student project that organizes computer science and related courses into clear pathways, helping students understand how classes connect to NOVA degrees, home school options, and IB programs.

Accessibility statement

The site is designed to meet WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines, including sufficient color contrast, keyboard navigability, descriptive link text, and semantic HTML structure.

  • Skip link to jump directly to main content.
  • Headings in logical order (h1, h2, h3).
  • Alt text for meaningful images.
  • High-contrast color palette and focus outlines.

Contact

For questions about the courses or pathways, please contact your teacher or counselor. This site is for informational and planning purposes only.
