Buffalo Chicken Dip

I chose this recipe because my mom consistently makes this recipe for myself and my siblings. It reminds me of when I was a kid. We usually eat this when football games are on. We all love this recipe. As my mom said"It's the best dip in the world."

Tips, Tricks, and Subsitutions

  • Use rotisserie chicken for more flavor
  • Shred chicken finely. Don't just seperate it with your hands and call it a day. Use a stand mixer or chop it up.
  • Soften your cream cheese first. Don't put it straight in cold. Make it warm to make the recipe faster and tastier
  • Personalize it. You can add other things to make it taste better. I'm not your boss. I'm here to help.
Ingredients: 1/2 Chicken, 12 fl oz Buffalo Sauce, 2 packets Cream Cheese, 2 cup Cheese
Step 1: Seperate chicken into smaller pieces
Step 2: Add chicken into a bowl
Step 3: Pour Buffalo Sauce all over the chicken
Step 4: Add cream cheese to the bowl
Step 5: Mix well
Step 6: Add cheese to the top
Step 7: Bake in the oven uncovered for about 20 minutes
Step 8: Take out of oven and garnish with green onions
Step 9: Wait for it to cool and serve with tortilla chips. Enjoy!
This recipe takes about 30-35 minutes and serves 4
